Overview of Webpage:

Roza, is a biological researcher in a prominant microbiology laboratory. Many bacterial species are not well studied and many more remain unknown to science. Roza's role is to discover and document these bacteria. In particular, Roza is interested in bacterial species that have the ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. Her lab has partnered with Improbable Beef, to research and find bacteria for this cause. Roza believes that the human belly button potentially holds the bacteria that Improbable Beef and many other organizations are looking for. Roza has sampled the navals of numerous volunteers. The dashboard on this webpage can be filtered to view and analyze the information for specific individuals.
